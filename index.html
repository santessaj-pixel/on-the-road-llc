Company Overview

On-The-Road, LLC, operating as OTR Courier Services, is a Virginia-based transportation and logistics company providing dependable non-CDL courier, delivery, messenger, and transportation support services throughout the Hampton Roads region.

We support government agencies, prime contractors, healthcare organizations, commercial businesses, community partners, and local customers that need reliable, professional, and responsive transportation solutions.
